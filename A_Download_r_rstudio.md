### macOS

1. Visit: [https://cran.r-project.org](https://cran.r-project.org)  
2. Click **Download R for macOS**.  
3. Download the latest `.pkg` file (e.g., *R-4.x.x.pkg*).  
4. Open the installer and follow the steps (use default settings).  
5. After installation, R will appear in your Applications folder and be ready for RStudio.
